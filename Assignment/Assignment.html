Welcome to the home of Balloon Keepy Uppy!

Here you can find all things regarding the MTU Balloon Keepy Uppy club. The navigation bar at the top of the page will bring you to any page of the site you are looking for. The navigation bar contains links to the events timetable, the signup form, the contact information page and of course a link back to the home page.


The club was set up this year by a of group students to provide a fun outlet for MTU students. During the Lockdowns of 2020 and 2021, students needed to find a way to stay busy. This is where the idea for Balloon Keepy Uppy club originated. The game went viral with challenges being posted on social media apps such as TikTok. Many students took part in these challenges to ease the lockdown boredom. Students found themselves thoroughly enjoying the game and wanting to expand into the world of team sport. The game has grown from strength to strength and teams have been set up in universities nationwide! The first Balloon Keepy Uppy World Cup was held this year in Spain. After a thrilling tournament Peru beat South American rivals Argentina in the final and took home the win!

Covid-19 is a prevalent problem in today's world. Due to this fact, protocols have been put in to place to protect all members of the Balloon Keepy Uppy team.
The following is a list of rules which must be followed:

  • Mask be worn when entering/leaving all events(including training)
  • Members must maintain social distancing at all events(excluding in games)
  • If you display any Covid-19 symptoms you are not to attend events
  • Covid Vaccination Certificates are required to enter events

These rules are in place to keep members safe and ensure the health of all who attend.
